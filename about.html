About Me

I'm actively involved in the IEEE Student Chapter and the Electronic ARTrium project, where I’ve contributed to automation and documentation efforts. I’ve also interned with HFCL and Promaxo, working on PoE modules and testing automation.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy art, collaborative projects, and exploring the intersection of hardware and intelligent systems.
